Momir Printer

Pick a mana value, roll a random creature, and print it on a thermal printer — just like the Momir format from Magic: The Gathering Online.

How to Use

  • Turn on your Phomemo printer
  • Click Connect and select your printer from the browser's Bluetooth picker
  • Pick a mana value (0–16) and hit Roll
  • Click Print to send the card to your printer

Settings

  • Auto-print — prints automatically after each roll
  • Include Un-sets — adds silver-bordered/funny cards to the pool
  • Print Art — includes dithered card artwork on the printout
  • Hide Preview — hides the card on screen for a surprise print

Supported Printers

  • Phomemo M02 (384 dots, 48mm)
  • Phomemo M02S (576 dots, 53mm)
  • Phomemo M02 Pro (576 dots, 53mm)
  • Phomemo T02 (384 dots, 48mm)

Browser Support

Requires Web Bluetooth — works in Chrome and Edge on Android and desktop. Not supported on iOS (Apple does not allow Web Bluetooth on any iOS browser).
